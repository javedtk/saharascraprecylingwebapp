About Us

Sahara Scrap Recycling Co. is a government-authorized and environmentally certified organization, dedicated to providing comprehensive industrial scrap management and sustainable waste recycling solutions. Headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, with a fully equipped processing facility at Panvel (Raigad District), we operate under the licenses of the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) and Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), along with statutory factory compliance.

Backed by years of industry experience, we specialize in the segregation, and recycling of ferrous and non-ferrous metal scrap, industrial plastic waste, paper and packaging waste, wooden scrap, thermocol (EPS), and wet/dry industrial waste, Our services are tailored to meet the compliance requirements of manufacturing industries, pharmaceutical companies, chemical units, IT facilities, and commercial establishments across India.

Our operations are designed to align with India’s evolving environmental policies, promoting circular economy principles by maximizing material recovery and minimizing landfill dependency. We offer end-to-end waste management solutions, from on-site scrap pickup and segregation to environmentally safe processing and documentation.

At Sahara Scrap Recycling Co., we believe in building long-term partnerships based on transparency, regulatory integrity, and operational excellence. Our professional team ensures that every step — from material collection to final recycling —both client expectations and statutory environmental standards.

To become India’s most trusted industrial waste management partner, by offering safe, ethical, and efficient recycling services, with full legal compliance, transparency, and traceability.
